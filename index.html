What is the acceleration due to gravity?
Acceleration is the rate of change of the velocity of an object with respect to time and the acceleration due to gravity is the acceleration gained by an object due to gravitational force.
How to calculate acceleration due to gravity?
To calculate acceleration due to gravity you should use the following formula:
f = (G * M) / r^2
Why use acceleration due to gravity?
The acceleration due to gravity is used to make satellites orbit around the planets and other celestial bodies.
